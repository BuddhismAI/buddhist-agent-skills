## Topic Criteria

- **Active** — has dedicated source collection(s) processed at depth, with topic index providing routing/concept map/correctness anchors. Synthesis docs added when 2+ collections exist.
- **Seed** — has aggregated cross-collection content with proper index, but no dedicated foundational text yet. Agent should answer from seed content + training knowledge with transparency about coverage limits.
- **Planned** — recognized as a needed topic, no curated content yet. Agent answers from training knowledge, transparent about gap.

A topic earns its own `index.md` when it has: its own source base, its own vocabulary, its own confusion patterns, and its own routing logic.
